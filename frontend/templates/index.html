How It Works Under the Hood

Every booking passes through three OS primitives — scoped per showtime so Inception in Hall A never blocks The Dark Knight in Hall B.

Mutex Lock

A threading.Lock() guards the critical section where seat states are read and written per showtime, preventing any race condition.

A threading.Semaphore(capacity) per hall enforces the physical seat ceiling — fully booked halls block at the gate.
